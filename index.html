This is how you play this game


  • The game is divided in three different rows, first player area, void and second player area
  • The goal is to clear your area from all the cards
  • Each player gets his turn to press a number which then is distributed one each on the following columsn in anti-clockwise order
  • The cards get distributed to opponent's area once enough number is clicked but ne point is lost every time the numbers have to pass through the ring
  • The game has lot of area for thinking and stratigizing
